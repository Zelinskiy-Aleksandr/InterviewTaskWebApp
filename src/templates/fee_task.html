You should test the form for cash withdrawal fee calculation.
Set "amount" and press "calc" to get the fee.

Calculation logic (in the cents):
range1 0 < amount <= 1 000 00 1.00%
range2 1 000 00 < amount <= 5 000 00 0.8%
range3 5 000 00 < amount <= 10 000 00 0.6%
range4 10 000 00 < amount 0.1%

If the withdrawal amount is 800.00 EUR (less than range1), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to range1 - 1.00%.
If the withdrawal amount is 1 000.00 EUR (equal to range1), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to range1 - 1.00%.
If the withdrawal amount is 5 500.00 EUR (more than range2, less than range3), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to range3 - 0.60%.
If the withdrawal amount is 150 000.00 EUR (more than range4), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to 0.10%.
