You should test the form for cash withdrawal fee calculation.
Set "amount" and press "calc" to get the fee.

Calculation logic (in the kopecks):
range1=1 000 00; perc1 = 1.00%;
range2=5 000 00; perc2 = 0.8%;
range3=10 000 00; perc3 = 0.6%;
range4 >10 000 00; perc4 = 0.1%;

If the withdrawal amount is 800.00 UAH (less than range1), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to range1 - 1.00%.
If the withdrawal amount is 1 000.00 UAH (equal to range1), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to range1 - 1.00%.
If the withdrawal amount is 5 500.00 UAH (more than range2, less than range3), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to range3 - 0.60%.
If the withdrawal amount is 150 000.00 UAH (more than range4), then the terminal will use commission corresponding to 0.10%.
